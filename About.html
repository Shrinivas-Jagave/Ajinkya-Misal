Our History

Ajinkya Misal began its journey on 23 March 2022, rooted in a deep passion for preserving the authentic taste of traditional Maharashtrian Misal. What started as a small, humble initiative soon gained recognition for its rich flavors, consistent quality, and dedication to tradition.

From 2022 onward, Ajinkya Misal focused on using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Through customer trust and unwavering commitment, the brand steadily grew into a respected name known for taste, hygiene, and reliability.

As of 2026, Ajinkya Misal proudly serves a growing community of loyal customers while continuing to honor its cultural heritage and culinary roots.
